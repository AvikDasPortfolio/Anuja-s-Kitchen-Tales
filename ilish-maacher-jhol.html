Ilish Maacher Jhol - Bengali Hilsa Curry

The legendary Hilsa fish curry, slow-cooked with mustard paste, green chilies, turmeric, and potatoes. This dish holds a special place in Bengali hearts, bringing together the rich aroma of fresh Hilsa and the earthy punch of mustard, served best with steamed rice. Price: 150/-
