Shukto – A Bengali Bitter-Sweet Classic

Shukto is a delicate Bengali vegetarian dish, known for its slightly bitter taste, usually made with bitter gourd, raw banana, drumsticks, and a mustard-poppy seed paste. Traditionally served at the beginning of a Bengali meal, Shukto is more than just a dish – it’s a cultural prelude to comfort and nostalgia. Try it with steamed rice for the most authentic experience!
